# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism (v3.9)
### **The Andy Warhol Edition**

**Style:** A **silk-screen print aesthetic** inspired by **Andy Warhol's factory work**. Features high-contrast images, intentional "registration errors" (color bleeding outside lines), and a synthetic, neon palette.

**The Vista:** **[City, Country]** is rendered as a high-contrast, two-tone silk-screened map. Landmarks are simplified into celebrity-style icons.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** The **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** appears as a **3x3 Repeating Grid** of identical icons, each in a different jarring neon color combination (e.g., Electric Blue/Hot Pink). 
* **Secondary:** Drifting ingredients are repeated icons, suggesting an infinite assembly line.
* **Shadows:** No natural shadows; scale is communicated by **flat color-offset blocks**.

**The Text:** The phrase **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"** is printed in a **stamped, repetitive font** across the sky, as if from a commercial rubber stamp.
