# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism (v4.1)
### **The Yayoi Kusama Edition**

**Style:** An **immersive installation aesthetic** inspired by **Yayoi Kusama**. Every element is "obliterated" by an endless pattern of **polka dots of varying sizes**.

**The Vista:** The aerial view of **[City, Country]** is entirely covered in a **yellow and black polka-dot pattern**. The landmarks appear to be melting into an infinite sea of dots.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** A city-sized **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** (shaped like a giant, organic pumpkin or soft-sculpture) covered in **dense, obsessive dots**. The "Life-Form" is a single figure peering into a **mirrored hole** in the side of the ingredient, seeing an infinity of dots.
* **Secondary:** Ingredients are "dot-moons."
* **Shadows:** Shadows are composed of **extremely dense, black-on-black polka dots**.

**The Text:** The phrase **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"** is written in a handwritten, obsessive script, with **dots filling the counters of every letter**.
