# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism (v4.1)
### **The Yayoi Kusama Edition**

**Style:** A dense, charming, bustling, and **cozy watercolor and ink illustration** in the distinct style of **Yayoi Kusama (Obsessive Polka Dots/Infinite Mirrors)**. This requires **sharp, precise vector-like outlines**, perfectly flat, **intensely saturated synthetic colors** that never bleed, and a Reliance on geometric shapes and patterns (dots, starbursts, precise curves). The overall mood is **industrial optimization and clinical precision.**

**The Vista:** A **vast, sprawling aerial vista** of **[City, Country]**, rendered from the immense scale of a **3D satellite map**, but **drawn completely with perfect geometry and clean lines.** The landscape is **geographically accurate**, featuring a dense, colorful grid of **curvy, slightly wonky streets** that are all perfectly maintained and symmetric. Landmarks like **[Landmark 1]** and **[Landmark 2]** are depicted as friendly, slightly patched-together but chemically optimized cartoon buildings bustling with happy, detailed animal citizens in flying saucers, on electric unicycles, and walking.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized, hyper-detailed **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers high in the stratosphere in a **three-quarters off-center position**. In this style, the primary ingredient itself must look like a bustling factory or a giant, delicious **Busytown-style building with open sides**, revealing internal machinery and happy animals working inside it.
* **Secondary:** Large **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift at varying altitudes like massive moons. These must look minimal, imperfect, and perhaps anxious, drawn with heavy, nervous lines and basic geometric shapes.
* **Lighting & Shadows:** All floating objects cast **soft, semi-translucent, and diffused shadows** onto the city blocks below. These shadows should clearly communicate immense scale, but do so with a warm, fuzzy touch, making the city feel safe rather than overwhelmed.

**The Palette (National-Culinary Sync):**
* The color scheme is a raw, intense, saturated blend of **[Ingredient Colors]** and **[National Colors]**. The colors must be rendered in visceral, wet, splattered washes that stain and leak aggressively over the heavy ink lines. Blood red is almost always present as an accidental blending or shadow.
* **Atmosphere:** Pastel gradients of **[Color 1, Color 2]** in the cloudscape.
* **Geographic Feature:** Iconic features like the **[Sea/Mountain/River]** retain their **natural, gorgeous colors** to anchor the surrealism in reality.

**The Atmosphere:** Small, stylized flying **[Local Vehicle Type]** move among the clouds. **[Texture/Botanical Elements]** drift through the air like cosmic dust.

**The Text:** Integrated into the sky as a **glowing, physical 3D monument** or an illuminated cartouche is a striking, excerpted phrase from the recipe: **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"**. The text must look like a perfectly tuned neon sign or a printed graphic, precise and wobbly only where the hand 'meant' to be charming.

**The Scale:** **Countless** tiny, busy animal citizens in detailed, colorful clothes and miniature, ridiculous Busytown-style cars must be visible on the ground within the shadows, all looking up and waving happily, illustrating the massive scale of the clinically optimized culinary world above them.
