# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism (v4.0)
### **The Takashi Murakami Edition**

**Style:** A complex, dynamic, **clinically precise and hyper-saturated illustration** in the combined style of **Takashi Murakami and modern *Otaku* Superflat art**. This requires **extreme, bold, vector-like black outlines**, perfectly flat, intensely saturated synthetic colors, zero texture or shading, and a composition entirely covered in manic, smiling flowers and pattern. Every object must be teeming with life and a slightly humorous visual gag.

**The Vista:** A **vast, sprawling aerial vista** of **[City, Country]**, rendered from the immense scale of a **3D satellite map**, but **drawn completely with perfect geometry and clean lines.** The landscape is **geographically accurate**, featuring a dense, colorful grid of **curvy, slightly wonky streets** and specific landmarks like **[Landmark 1]** and **[Landmark 2]**, all depicting smiling, manic flower-citizens in cars, on bicycles, and walking.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers high in the stratosphere in a **three-quarters off-center position**. In this style, the primary ingredient is not a natural object, but a **optimized industrial part.** If tuna cubes, they are not natural cubes but **perfectly engineered Tuna-Gems**, glistening with synthesized sriracha-mayo lacquer and perfect geometric sesame dots, appearing to have been assembled with robotic care.
* **Secondary:** Large **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift at varying altitudes like massive moons. These must look minimal, imperfect, and perhaps anxious, drawn with heavy, nervous lines and basic geometric shapes.
* **Lighting & Shadows:** All floating objects cast **soft, semi-translucent, and diffused shadows** onto the city blocks below. These shadows should clearly communicate immense scale, but do so with a warm, fuzzy touch, making the city feel safe rather than overwhelmed.

**The Palette (National-Culinary Sync):**
* The color scheme is a warm, cozy blend of **Guava Pink, Espresso Brown, and Colombian National Colors (Yellow, Blue, Red)**. The primary mood is **bright, friendly, and sugary.**
* **Atmosphere:** Pastel gradients of **[Color 1, Color 2]** in the cloudscape.
* **Geographic Feature:** Iconic features like the **[Sea/Mountain/River]** retain their **natural, gorgeous colors** to anchor the surrealism in reality.

**The Atmosphere:** Flying **[Local Vehicle Type]** move among the clouds. **Translucent botanical elements** drift through the air like **smiling, winking cosmic dust** or giant, happy seeds.

**The Text:** Integrated into the sky as a **glowing, physical 3D monument** made of clear, flowing script is the text: **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"**.

**The Scale:** **Tiny, ant-like figures and miniature cars** must be visible on the ground within the soft shadows of the floating ingredients to emphasize the cosmic scale.
