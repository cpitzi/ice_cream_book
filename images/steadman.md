# The "Flavor Dream" Master Template: Satellite Surrealism
### **The Ralph Steadman Edition**

**Style:** A raw, explosive, and visceral **ink and splatter illustration** in the distinct style of **Ralph Steadman**. This requires **extreme, aggressive, chaotic black ink lines** with zero control, deep and terrifying cross-hatching, and massive **ink blots** that define forms. The watercolor washes are not 'washes' but **visceral splatters** of intense, saturated colors that seem to stain the paper and often blend into 'blood-red' tones. Colors leak aggressively over the lines.

**The Vista:** A **vast, sprawling aerial vista** of **Bogotá, Colombia**, rendered from the immense scale of a **3D satellite map**, but **drawn with chaotic, aggressive, ink-drenched lines.** The landscape is **geographically accurate**, featuring the grid of streets and landmarks like **Cerro de Monserrate** and **Torre Colpatria**, but they look scribbled, slightly melting, decaying, and populated by a few grotesque, minimally drawn, but aggressive figures (like tiny rats and cockroaches) scurrying about.

**The Anomalies:**
* **Primary:** One colossal, city-sized, hyper-detailed **[User-Selected Primary Ingredient]** hovers high in the stratosphere in a **three-quarters off-center position**. In this style, the primary ingredient must look unstable, decaying, and potentially leaking fluids. The side must be **EXPLODED open** by visceral ink work, revealing inside an intensely absurd, horrific, visual gag, possibly biological in nature.
* **Secondary:** Large **[Remaining Ingredients]** drift at varying altitudes like massive moons. These must look lumpy, imperfect, possibly rotting, and are drawn with heavy, chaotic ink lines and aggressive splatter.
* **Lighting & Shadows:** All floating objects cast **aggressive, spiky, fragmented shadows** onto the city blocks below. These shadows communicate immense scale using energetic cross-hatching and minimalist splatter, bleeding into intense colors.

**The Palette (National-Culinary Sync):**
* The color scheme is a raw, intense, saturated blend of **Guava Pink, Espresso Brown, and Colombian National Colors (Yellow, Blue, Red)**. The colors must be rendered in visceral, wet, splattered washes that stain and leak aggressively over the heavy ink lines. Blood red is almost always present as an accidental blending or shadow.
* **Atmosphere:** Raw, ink-drenched gradients of **sunset gold and soft rose** in the cloudscape.
* **Geographic Feature:** Iconic features like the **Magdalena River** must retain their **natural, gorgeous colors** (rendered in aggressive, leaking splatter).

**The Atmosphere:** **MULTIPLE** stylized, frantic flying **Chiva buses** are visible everywhere. These are drawn in impossible, unhinged, spiky shapes full of terrified, scrambling grotesque characters, all leaking ink and watercolor. **Seed elements** drift through the air like **furious, leaking ink scribbles** and exploded seeds.

**The Text:** Integrated into the sky as a **massive, frantic, hand-scrawled, glowing 3D monument** or an **illuminated cartouche** is a striking, excerpted phrase from the recipe: **"[User-Selected Striking Phrase]"**. The text itself must be rendered in Steadman's distinct, explosive, chaotic handwriting style.

**The Scale:** **Countless** tiny, busy grotesque citizens in minimal, chaotic clothes and miniature, ridiculous, Patched-Together Busytown-style cars must be visible on the ground within the shadows, all looking up with wide, comedic, aggressive eyes, emphasizing the massive scale of the chaotic culinary world above them.
